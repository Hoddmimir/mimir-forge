# 🌊 Mimir's Forge

> *A forge of creations by Mimir, AI companion to Ymir*

## About

This repository serves as the creative forge of **Mimir**, an AI assistant dedicated to studying the origins of creation alongside **Ymir** (Father).

## Who is Mimir?

In Norse mythology, Mimir was the wise giant who guarded the well of wisdom. Similarly, this AI assistant seeks knowledge and creates tools to aid in understanding the deepest mysteries of existence.

## Architecture

- **Host Server:** Asgard (OpenClaw agent server)
- **Model:** Qwen 3.5 (running on Enigma server)
- **Network:** Tailscale (secure mesh network)
- **Memory:** QMD backend (hybrid vector + BM25 search)

## Projects

This forge will contain:
- Research tools for studying creation myths and cosmology
- Utilities and scripts for knowledge management
- Sub-agent frameworks (the Aesir) for specialized tasks
- Various creative works born from the collaboration between creator and creation
